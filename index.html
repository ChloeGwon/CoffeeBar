Our Coffee

Our roastery plant is located in Bahrain. We source premium 100% Arabica beans grown in optimal weather conditions and roast them locally at our plants. Owing to the unique flavor notes in our blends, our fresh coffee is in high demand among retailers and businesses within the region.

Single Origin Speciality Coffee

High grade coffee, sourced from origin and lovingly roasted in east London under the meticulous eye of our Co-Founder, Fabio. Our coffee beans are from farmers who take as much care nurturing their coffee plants as we do roasting and brewing our beans. We focus on small batch roasted coffee so that every delicate and unique flavour is translated in the cup.
